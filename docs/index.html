Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

General

This Privacy Policy describes how GeoTrack collects, uses and protects personal data.

🔐 Your GPS coordinates are encrypted with AES-256 directly on your device before being sent to the server.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — for account creation via Firebase Authentication
  • Display name — shown to group members on the map
  • Location data — GPS coordinates encrypted with AES-256 on your device
  • Location history — encrypted coordinates for the last 24 hours
  • FCM token — for push notifications about geozones

Encryption

Coordinates are encrypted on your device with a unique key per group and a random IV per update. The server never sees real coordinates.

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Unique AES-256 key per group
  • Random initialization vector per update
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict access to group members only

How We Use Data

  • Show your location to group members on the map
  • Send geozone entry/exit notifications
  • Store location history for later viewing
  • Authentication and account management

❌ We never sell or share your data for advertising purposes.

Third Parties

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and encrypted data storage
  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles display

Your Rights

  • Disable location tracking at any time
  • Delete your location data via Privacy settings
  • Hide yourself from the map
  • Request account deletion — contact us by email

Children

GeoTrack is intended for users aged 13 and over. The Parent Mode allows parents to track children's location within a family group with their consent.
